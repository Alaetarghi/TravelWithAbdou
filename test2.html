Morocco Highlights Tour

9 Days / 8 Nights – Imperial Cities, Sahara & Atlantic Coast

Why You’ll Love This Journey

Imperial Cities

Discover Fes, Marrakech & Meknes, rich in history, culture and architecture.

Sahara Desert

Camel ride, sunset over golden dunes, Berber music under the stars.

Atlas Mountains

Breathtaking landscapes crossing the Middle & High Atlas.

Atlantic Coast

Relax in Essaouira, a charming seaside town with artistic vibes.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival Casablanca → Chefchaouen

Arrival at Casablanca Airport, then drive north to the blue city of Chefchaouen. Free evening to explore the medina.

Overnight: Riad Dar M&D – Chefchaouen

Day 2 – Chefchaouen → Volubilis → Meknes → Fes

Visit Roman ruins of Volubilis (UNESCO), Meknes historical sites, then drive to Fes.
Overnight: Dar Victoria or Riad Mebrouka – Fes

Day 3 – Guided Tour of Fes

Explore the UNESCO medina, Al Quaraouiyine University, tanneries, souks & madrasas.
Overnight: Dar Victoria or Riad Mebrouka – Fes

Day 4 – Fes → Ifrane → Azrou → Merzouga

Drive through the Middle Atlas, cedar forests, then camel ride into the Sahara at sunset.
Overnight: Sahara Stars Camp – Merzouga

Day 5 – Merzouga → Todra Gorges → Ait Benhaddou

Sunrise over dunes, Todra Gorges, Dades Valley and Kasbah Ait Benhaddou (UNESCO).
Overnight: Kasbah Titrit – Ait Benhaddou

Day 6 – Ait Benhaddou → Ouarzazate → Marrakech

Cross the High Atlas via Tizi n’Tichka pass toward vibrant Marrakech.
Overnight: Riad Arabkech – Marrakech

Day 7 – Marrakech City Tour

Bahia Palace, Saadian Tombs, Koutoubia Mosque, souks & Jemaa El Fna.
Overnight: Riad Arabkech – Marrakech

Day 8 – Marrakech → Essaouira

Discover Essaouira medina, port, ramparts & beach.
Overnight: La Maison du Sud or Palais des Remparts

Day 9 – Departure

Transfer to Essaouira, Marrakech or Casablanca Airport.
